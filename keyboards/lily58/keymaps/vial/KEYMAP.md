# Lily58 Unified Keymap (BASE/NAV/NUM/SYS)

This keymap is designed to mirror the ZMK Sofle configuration, providing a consistent experience across both keyboards.

## Layers

1.  **`_BASE`**: Standard QWERTY with homerow mods.
2.  **`_NAV`**: Navigation, mouse controls, and symbols.
3.  **`_NUM`**: F-keys and a right-hand numpad.
4.  **`_SYS`**: System commands, firmware settings (`QK_BOOT`), and holykeebs (`HK_*`) macros.

---
